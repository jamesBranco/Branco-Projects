Cilantro Sauce

Green Sauce or Cilantro Sauce is a very popular, and must have home-made sauce in any Brazilian cookout. More than that, is very simple and really tasty. It is made out of some fresh herbs and some spices. Feel free to add or remove any you want spice! It is served with churrasco (grilled meats), rice, or any other savory food or snack you want.

    • Ingredientes

    • 1 Lime or Lemon
    • 2 Cloves of Garlic
    • 1/2 Onion
    • 1/2 Tsp of Oregano
    • 500 Grams of Mayonnaise
    • 1/2 Cup of Olive Oil
    • 1 Bunch of Cilantro
    • Salt to Taste
    • Black Peper to Taste

    Equipments:

    • Blender

    • Long Spoon

    Instructions

    1. In a blender, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, oregano, black pepper, and cilantro
    2. Blend the ingredients until the mixture is smooth and homogeneous.
    3. Once blended, turn off the blender, add the mayonnaise, and blend again until the mixture is fully combined and smooth.
    4. Transfer the mixture to a bowl, and it's ready to serve.
