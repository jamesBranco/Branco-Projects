Brigadeiro

The Brazilian dessert Brigadeiro is a rich, chocolatey treat made from condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter, rolled into bite-sized balls and coated with sprinkles.

    • Ingredientes

    • 1 box or 395 grams of condensed milk
    • 1 tablespoon of unsalted margarine
    • 30 grams or 4 tablespoon of chocolate powder
    • 150 grams chocolate sprinkles

    Equipments:

    • Small Pot
    • Measuring Cup
    • Flat Plate
    • Spatula

    Instructions

    1. In a deep pan, combine the condensed milk, margarine, and chocolate powder.
    2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and starts to pull away from the sides of the pan.
    3. Allow the mixture to cool completely.
    4. Once cooled, use your hands to form small balls and roll them in chocolate sprinkles until fully coated.
